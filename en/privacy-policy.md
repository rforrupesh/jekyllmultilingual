# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** July 8, 2026

Welcome to UnificarPDF. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your information when you use our website and online PDF tools.

By using UnificarPDF, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

UnificarPDF is designed to minimize the collection of personal information.

Depending on how you use our services, we may collect:

* Files and images uploaded for processing.
* Technical information such as browser type, operating system, device information, and IP address.
* Anonymous usage statistics to improve our services.
* Cookies and similar technologies for website functionality, analytics, and advertising.

## 2. File Processing

Files uploaded to UnificarPDF are processed solely to provide the requested service.

* Files are uploaded directly by users from their own devices.
* We do not claim ownership of uploaded files.
* Uploaded files are not permanently stored.
* Processed files are automatically deleted after processing or within a reasonable period necessary to provide the requested service.
* We do not access, review, or share the contents of uploaded files except when required for technical, security, or legal reasons.

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

* Provide and maintain our services.
* Improve website performance and user experience.
* Monitor service reliability and security.
* Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized use.
* Comply with applicable legal obligations.

## 4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to:

* Remember user preferences.
* Analyze website traffic.
* Improve website functionality.
* Display relevant advertisements.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, although some features may not function properly.

## 5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for analytics, website hosting, advertising, and performance monitoring.

These services may include, but are not limited to:

* **Google AdSense**, to display advertisements on our website.
* **Google Analytics**, to collect anonymous website usage statistics.
* **Google Search Console**, to monitor our website's performance in Google Search.

These providers may process limited technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

For more information, please visit:

* https://policies.google.com/privacy
* https://policies.google.com/technologies
* https://support.google.com/analytics

## 6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect user information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed toward children under the age required by applicable law.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Users remain solely responsible for all files uploaded to our services.

By uploading content, you confirm that you have the legal right to use, process, and convert the files.

UnificarPDF does not monitor user content and does not assume responsibility for copyright violations committed by users.

## 9. Legal Compliance

We may disclose information when required by applicable law, court order, legal process, or governmental request.

## 10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
