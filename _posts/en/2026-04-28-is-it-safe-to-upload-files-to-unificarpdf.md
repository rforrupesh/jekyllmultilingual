What exactly happens to your files when you use our website? Is there anything you should worry about? The short answer is no, absolutely not. However, we prefer to provide more information rather than simply answering the question. Here's why.

## About Us

Our application was created with a single goal: to make [PDF editing](/en/blog/how-to-use-unificarpdf-com/) simple and accessible. Our audience includes secretaries, students, lawyers, teachers, and even small business owners. Every person who uses our service deserves our attention and respect.

## Your File Is Automatically Protected Once You Upload It

The moment you choose a file, it is transferred through our secure HTTPS connection, which operates on the same principles used by online banking systems and healthcare networks. No one can intercept, access, or decode your information while it is being transmitted.

We avoid using the HTTPS protocol because it lacks security protections and would allow anyone monitoring the network to read the transmitted information.

## No One at UniFicarPDF Can View Your File

Neither our developers, support team, nor any other staff members have access to your file. The software processes your document automatically and returns the result directly to you.

## How Long Will My File Be Stored?

The answer is simple: only until your PDF processing is complete. You will have enough time to download your processed file before it is permanently deleted from our server. No copies are created, and we do not duplicate your documents. Once the retention period expires, the file is permanently removed.

You can also choose to delete the file yourself immediately after processing if you prefer.

*This means that everything related to your file happens exclusively on the server during processing and is removed afterward.*
