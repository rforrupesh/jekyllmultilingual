## What is a PDF?

PDF (Portable Document Format) keeps your layout exactly the same on every device. Great for sharing final documents.

## What is a Word document?

Word (.docx) files are editable and great for drafting, collaboration, and track changes.

## Which should you use?

- **Sharing final versions** → PDF
- **Editing and collaboration** → Word
- **Forms and contracts** → PDF
- **Drafts and reports** → Word
